Lasagna

Home Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish consisting of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with ragù (meat and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, cheeses like mozzarella, parmesan, or ricotta, and seasonings. Originating in Naples, this "al forno" (oven-baked) casserole is known for its rich, layered structure and is often finished with a crispy top layer of cheese.

Ingredients

    500g beef mince, 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 can (400g) crushed or diced tomatoes, 2 tbsp tomato paste, 1 tsp dried oregano, 1 tsp dried basil or Italian herbs, salt, pepper, olive oil, 250g ricotta or cottage cheese, 1 cup grated mozzarella, ½ cup grated parmesan, 1 egg optional, lasagna sheets

Steps

    1, Preheat oven to 180°C 2, Heat olive oil in a pan, cook onion until soft 3, Add garlic and cook for 1 minute 4, Add beef mince and cook until browned 5, Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, salt and pepper, simmer 10–15 minutes 6, In a bowl mix ricotta, egg optional, and a little salt 7, In a baking dish spread a layer of meat sauce 8, Add lasagna sheets 9, Spread ricotta mixture 10, Sprinkle mozzarella 11, Repeat layers finishing with meat sauce and mozzarella on top 12, Bake 30 minutes until golden and bubbling 13, Rest 10 minutes before slicing
